Omelette Recipe - Responsive

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cock

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
    room temperature
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

  • Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy.
  • Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the buffer.
  • Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.
  • Use a spatula to push the cooked eggs from the edge of the skillet toward...
  • When the eggs are mostly set but Still soft, fold in half.
  • Slide the omelette onto a plate.
